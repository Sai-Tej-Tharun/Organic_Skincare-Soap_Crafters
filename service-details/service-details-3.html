About This Workshop

GlowVeda's DIY Herbal Skincare Workshops are immersive, hands-on experiences held at our Thrissur studio. Led by our master herbalists with a combined 25+ years of experience, each workshop teaches you to create real, effective skincare products from scratch — using the same certified organic ingredients we use in our own product line.

Workshops run every Saturday and Sunday, with both half-day (3 hours) and full-day (6 hours) options. You don't need any prior experience — our beginner workshops start from the absolute basics, while our advanced sessions are designed for those who already make skincare at home and want to elevate their craft.

Every participant takes home all products they make during the session, a comprehensive recipe booklet with 20+ formulas, and a certificate of completion. We also offer private group workshops for birthdays, bachelorettes, corporate team-building events, and school/college programmes.

What You'll Learn & Make

  • Cold-process herbal soap bars using traditional saponification
  • Nourishing face serums with vitamin C, niacinamide and botanical extracts
  • Whipped body butters using shea, mango butter and essential oils
  • Ayurvedic hair oils infused with bhringraj, amla and hibiscus
  • Natural lip balms and tinted lip butters with cocoa and beeswax
  • Understanding oil chemistry and ingredient safety for home formulation
  • Preservative systems and shelf-life management for natural products
  • Labelling, packaging and storing your handmade products

How a Workshop Day Works

Step 01

Registration & Welcome

Arrive at our Thrissur studio and be welcomed with a herbal tea and a tour of our ingredient pantry. Your workstation will be set up with all the tools and pre-weighed ingredients for the day's recipes.

Step 02

Ingredient Science Talk (30 mins)

Our herbalist introduces the key ingredients for the session — where they come from, how they work on the skin, why we've chosen each one, and important safety notes for working at home.

Step 03

Hands-On Making (2–4 hrs)

Roll up your sleeves! You'll make each product with step-by-step guidance. Our instructors circulate to assist, answer questions and share tips. Every mistake is a learning moment — we've seen them all!

Step 04

Label, Package & Take Home

Once your products are made, you'll label and package them beautifully in GlowVeda's signature eco-kraft wrapping to take home. Your recipe booklet and completion certificate are distributed before you leave.

Step 05

Ongoing Support

All workshop alumni get access to our private WhatsApp community where you can share your home batches, ask questions, and get feedback from our instructors and fellow makers.
