About This Service

No two people have the same skin — and Ayurveda understood this 5,000 years ago. GlowVeda's Ayurvedic Skincare Consultation is a deeply personalised 45-minute session with our certified specialist who assesses your unique body constitution (Prakriti), identifies any Dosha (Vata, Pitta or Kapha) imbalances currently affecting your skin, and builds a comprehensive care protocol around them.

Unlike generic skin-type questionnaires, our consultation takes a holistic view — factoring in your diet, lifestyle, sleep patterns, stress levels, climate, and seasonal changes — to give you recommendations that work in the long term, not just as a temporary fix.

Every consultation concludes with a written Skincare Protocol document detailing your morning and evening routine, recommended GlowVeda products, dietary tips, and Ayurvedic lifestyle adjustments. You also receive a 15-day follow-up check-in call to review progress and refine the plan.

The Three Doshas We Assess

Our specialist evaluates all three Ayurvedic Doshas and their influence on your skin.

Vata Dosha Dry, flaky, rough or sensitive skin with fine lines
Pitta Dosha Oily, acne-prone, redness, hyperpigmentation, rosacea
Kapha Dosha Congested pores, dull skin, excess oil, water retention
Tridoshic All three Doshas in balance — maintenance and prevention

How the Consultation Works

Step 01

Pre-Session Questionnaire

After booking, you'll receive a detailed intake form covering your health history, current skincare routine, lifestyle habits, diet, and skin concerns. Complete this 24 hours before your session so our specialist can prepare.

Step 02

Prakriti & Skin Assessment

During your 45-minute session, our specialist conducts a thorough Prakriti analysis — examining your skin visually (and in-person, through touch tests), reviewing your questionnaire, and asking detailed questions about seasonal changes in your skin.

Step 03

Protocol Creation

Within 24 hours of your session, we email your personalised written Skincare Protocol — a detailed AM/PM routine, recommended GlowVeda products, dietary guidance, Ayurvedic herbs to incorporate, and lifestyle adjustments.

Step 04

15-Day Follow-Up

At the 15-day mark, you'll receive a check-in call to review progress, answer any questions, and fine-tune your protocol based on how your skin has responded. This call is included in all consultation tiers.
