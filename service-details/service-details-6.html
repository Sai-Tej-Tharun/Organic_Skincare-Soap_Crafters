About This Service

GlowVeda's Gifting Service takes the stress out of thoughtful gifting. We design, assemble and deliver custom organic skincare hampers for every occasion — from intimate personal gifts for a friend to large-scale corporate gifting campaigns for hundreds of employees or clients.

Every hamper is assembled by hand in our studio, using our full-size certified organic products. Nothing here is cheap or filler — each item in your kit is something the recipient will genuinely use and love. We offer three ready-curated kit sizes (Essential, Signature and Luxury) or you can build your own bespoke hamper by choosing individual products.

Our packaging is 100% plastic-free. Every kit comes in a rigid eco-kraft gift box with shredded seed paper packing (which the recipient can plant in their garden), a gold-foil GlowVeda seal, and a personalised handwritten or printed message card. Corporate orders of 50+ kits qualify for full co-branding including your company logo on the box and a branded insert card.

How to Order Gift Kits

Step 01

Choose Your Kit or Build Your Own

Select from our three curated kit sizes (Essential ₹499, Signature ₹999, Luxury ₹1,999), or build a completely bespoke hamper by choosing individual products from our full catalogue. For corporate orders, we recommend a discovery call to understand your brand and recipient profile.

Step 02

Personalise Your Message & Branding

Provide your personalised message card text (or our team can help write it). For corporate orders of 50+, share your logo and brand colours for co-branded packaging. Preview your box design before approval.

Step 03

Confirm & Pay

Submit your recipient list (for direct-to-door delivery) or a single delivery address. Pay securely via UPI, NEFT, credit/debit card. For corporate orders above ₹1 lakh, we offer Net-15 payment terms with a signed purchase order.

Step 04

We Assemble with Care

Every kit is hand-assembled by our gifting team in our Thrissur studio. Products are inspected for quality, arranged beautifully, wrapped in seed paper, sealed with a gold foil sticker, and packaged in your eco-kraft gift box.

Step 05

Delivered to Their Door

Kits are dispatched within 5–10 business days (rush orders available). Pan-India delivery via premium courier. For corporate multi-address delivery, we send a consolidated tracking sheet so you know the status of every kit.
