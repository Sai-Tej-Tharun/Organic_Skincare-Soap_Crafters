About the Subscription Box

The GlowVeda Subscription Box is the easiest way to maintain a consistent, effective Ayurvedic skincare routine. Every month, our curation team hand-picks 4–6 full-size products specifically suited to your skin type (oily, dry, combination, sensitive or acne-prone) and the current season — because Ayurveda recognises that your skin's needs shift with the weather.

Each box includes a handwritten ingredient story card explaining why each product was chosen for you, a seasonal DIY recipe to try at home, and at least one subscriber-exclusive formula — a product we create specifically for our box subscribers that is never available in our regular shop.

We use zero plastic in our packaging. Every box is made from recycled kraft board, and each product is wrapped in seed paper that you can plant in your garden. Your subscription also earns GlowVeda Glow Points — redeemable against future purchases.

How the Subscription Works

Step 01

Complete Your Skin Profile

At sign-up, you'll answer a short 8-question skin profile survey covering your skin type, main concerns, ingredients you love or avoid, and fragrance preferences. This takes less than 2 minutes.

Step 02

Our Team Curates Your Box

By the 25th of each month, our curation team finalises the upcoming box based on the season, trending Ayurvedic ingredients, and your individual profile. Subscriber exclusives are crafted in limited batches.

Step 03

Box Ships Between 1st–5th

Your box is packed, sealed with seed paper, and dispatched between the 1st and 5th of each month via our express courier partners. You'll receive a tracking link via WhatsApp and email.

Step 04

Unbox, Use & Share

Open your box, read your ingredient story card, and start your new ritual. Tag us @GlowVeda on Instagram with your unboxing — subscribers who share get bonus Glow Points!

Step 05

Update Your Profile Anytime

Has your skin changed? Moving to a new city? Just update your skin profile in your subscriber dashboard before the 20th of any month and we'll adjust the next box accordingly.
