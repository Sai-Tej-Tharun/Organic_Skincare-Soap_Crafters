About This Service

GlowVeda's Wholesale & Bulk Supply service is designed for businesses that want to offer genuinely natural, certified organic skincare to their clientele — without the overhead of in-house formulation. We supply spas, wellness retreats, boutique hotels, Ayurvedic clinics, pharmacies, organic grocery stores, and online retailers across India and internationally.

All GlowVeda products are manufactured in our ISO 22716 (Cosmetic GMP) compliant facility in Thrissur. Every batch undergoes internal quality testing and third-party lab verification before dispatch. Our full ingredient transparency policy means you receive a complete INCI list, CoA (Certificate of Analysis) and MSDS sheet with every order.

We offer three supply models: ready-to-ship branded stock (our existing product range with GlowVeda branding), white-label (our formulas with your branding), and private label (completely custom formulas developed to your brief). Dedicated account managers are assigned to all partners with monthly orders exceeding ₹50,000.

Wholesale Partnership Process

Step 01

Submit Wholesale Enquiry

Complete the enquiry form with your business details, the products you're interested in, required quantities, and whether you need branded, white-label, or private label supply.

Step 02

Receive Custom Quote

Our B2B team responds within 24 hours with a tiered pricing sheet, product catalogue, compliance documentation, and a sample kit offer (₹999, refundable on first order).

Step 03

Sample Approval

Review your sample kit, evaluate quality and fit for your clientele. For white-label orders, this phase includes label design review and packaging approval before production begins.

Step 04

Place Purchase Order

Sign a B2B supply agreement, submit your PO, and pay 50% advance. Our production team schedules your batch immediately. Balance payment is due before dispatch.

Step 05

Production, QC & Dispatch

Your order is produced, quality-checked, and documented. CoA, MSDS and INCI sheets are emailed on dispatch day. Your dedicated account manager shares a shipment tracking link via WhatsApp.
