About This Service

GlowVeda's Custom Soap Crafting service invites you to become the co-creator of your very own skin ritual. Rather than buying off-the-shelf soap bars, you get to handpick every element — from the base oil blend and active botanicals down to the essential oil fragrance, natural colourant, decorative topping, shape, and custom label.

Every bar is made using the traditional cold-process method, which preserves the full therapeutic potency of each botanical ingredient. Unlike commercially produced soaps that are boiled and stripped of natural glycerin, our cold-process bars retain all the goodness your skin deserves.

This service is ideal for personal use, thoughtful gifting, bridal favours, baby showers, corporate wellness kits, or hotel amenities. We cater to individuals, event planners, and businesses alike, with flexible pricing tiers starting from just ₹299 per bar for orders of 12 and above.

How the Process Works

Step 01

Design Your Formula

Fill in our online customisation form or visit our Thrissur studio. Choose your base oils (coconut, olive, castor, shea), active botanicals, essential oil blend, natural colourant, and any special additives like oatmeal, coffee grounds, or dried flower petals.

Step 02

Review & Confirm

Our head herbalist, Priya, personally reviews your formula for skin-safety and ingredient synergy. We'll send you a digital formula card within 24 hours for your approval, along with a scent sample option for fragrance choices.

Step 03

Cold-Process Crafting

Once approved, our artisans weigh, blend, and pour your formula using the cold-process method. The bars are poured into custom moulds, left to saponify for 24 hours, and unmoulded by hand.

Step 04

Curing Period

Each bar is cured for a minimum of 3 days to ensure the saponification process is complete and the bar is firm, long-lasting and gentle. Rush orders can be accommodated with our accelerated-cure process.

Step 05

Label, Package & Deliver

Finished bars are wrapped in our signature eco-kraft paper, labelled with your custom design or our standard GlowVeda branding, boxed in eco-friendly packaging, and dispatched via our courier partners within 3–5 business days.
