Our Origin

A Recipe Passed Down Through Generations

GlowVeda was born in 2016 in the kitchen of our founder, Priya Nair, inspired by her grandmother's handwritten notebook of Ayurvedic home remedies. Growing up watching her grandmother grind turmeric, extract neem oils, and blend sandalwood pastes, Priya understood early that nature held the most powerful skincare secrets.

Frustrated by the chemical overload in mainstream beauty products, Priya began crafting small batches of herbal soaps for family and friends. Word spread quickly — and GlowVeda was born. What started as a passion project in a 200 sq ft kitchen has grown into a beloved brand trusted by over 50,000 customers across India.

Every GlowVeda product carries the same philosophy: what you put on your skin should be as pure as what you put in your body. No parabens, no sulphates, no synthetic fragrances — just the honest goodness of nature, carefully crafted by hand.

The Person Behind GlowVeda

Meet Priya Nair,
Founder & Chief Herbalist

"I didn't start GlowVeda to build a company. I started it to solve a problem — my own skin. Everything else followed naturally, just like the ingredients we use."

Priya holds a postgraduate degree in Ayurvedic Sciences from the Vaidyaratnam Oushadhasala in Kerala and spent three years studying traditional soap-making with master craftspeople in Mysore and Kashi.

Her philosophy is simple: every GlowVeda formula must pass the "grandmother test" — if her grandmother wouldn't recognise every ingredient on the label, it doesn't belong in the product.

Today, Priya leads a team of 18 passionate artisans, herbalists, and wellness advocates, and personally reviews every new formulation before it reaches your skin.

From a kitchen counter to thousands of homes across India.

Priya crafts her first batch of turmeric-neem soap from her grandmother's recipe, sharing it with friends and family. 50 bars. Zero marketing. Word of mouth only.

First Organic Market

GlowVeda debuts at the Kerala Organic Living Festival. Sells out all 500 units in 3 hours. First press mention in The Hindu's wellness supplement.

Farm to Skin

Where Our Ingredients Come From

We believe transparency starts at the source. Every herb, oil, and botanical in your GlowVeda product has a traceable origin and a farmer behind it.

Wayanad, Kerala

Turmeric & Neem

Sourced from certified organic farms in Wayanad, where our partner farmers have cultivated these herbs for three generations using traditional methods.

Jodhpur, Rajasthan

Aloe Vera

Our cold-pressed aloe gel comes from Jodhpur's sun-rich organic plantations, harvested at peak potency for maximum skin-soothing benefits.

Mysore, Karnataka

Sandalwood

Sustainably harvested sandalwood from Karnataka's government-approved plantations, steam-distilled for pure, therapeutic-grade essential oil.

Kannauj, UP

Rose & Herbs

The perfume capital of India, Kannauj, supplies our rose water and floral extracts — distilled using century-old deg-bhapka techniques.

Our Sourcing Commitment

We pay fair-trade prices to all partner farms, visit every farm at least once a year, and maintain a fully traceable supply chain. If we can't verify an ingredient's origin, it doesn't enter our products.
